## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is MatchPic?

MatchPic is an AI-powered photo generation tool that helps users create personalized portraits and profile photos.

---

### Is MatchPic a social networking app?

No.

MatchPic is a photo creation and AI portrait enhancement tool.

The app does not provide:

- Messaging features
- User-to-user communication
- Social networking services
- User discovery
- Matching systems

---

### Are my photos used to train AI models?

No.

Your uploaded photos are not used to train AI models.

AI generation time may vary depending on processing conditions. Most images are created within a few minutes.
